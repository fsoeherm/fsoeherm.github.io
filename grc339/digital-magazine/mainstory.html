Featured Designer: Dani Klaric

Gen-Z sensation turned interior design powerhouse! From TikTok to owning her own firm, she rocks art-deco style with vintage vibes. Dani's secret? Second-hand treasures and timeless patterns.

Dani Klaric is a Mexican interior decorator known for her whimsical and eclectic decor style. Specialized in residential and commercial spaces and documents her love for design and cinematography on Various Social Media Platforms.

Dani Klaric works on commercial and residential interiors and projects showing her skills with all sorts of styles, ranging from mid-century modern, minimalist pieces, whimsical homes, and fairy aesthetics. Her work is truly one of a kind. The spaces she transforms typically bring such a whimsical personality.

This creative started in the entertainment industry and pivoting when realizing how much she had loved set design and not working a camera.

Her bubbly personality has really helped grow her following on TikTok - her mainstream platform where you shares how she tranforms her own home, a tease at current projects, and more. Dani Klaric also hosts a podcast called Accidental Experts, with TayBeepBoop, a fellow interior designer who is also very skillful in DIYs. Accidental Experts is where Dani Klaric and Tay Beep Boop interview those in the creative field who have an uncommon career, typically one they set to create for themselves.
