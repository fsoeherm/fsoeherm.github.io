About us

Welcome to Broth & Board, a unique restaurant that specializes in two of the most comforting and flavorful culinary delights: soups and charcuterie boards. Our mission is to provide our guests to come together with their loved ones with a warm and inviting atmosphere where they can savor the rich and complex flavors of our carefully crafted soups and indulge in the savory delights of our charcuterie boards.

At Broth & Board, we pride ourselves on our use of fresh, locally sourced ingredients to create our soups and charcuterie boards. Our chefs are passionate about experimenting with bold flavor combinations and unique ingredients to create dishes that are both familiar and exciting.

Whether you're in the mood for a hearty bowl of soup or a charcuterie board filled with an array of cured meats, cheeses, and other delectable treats, we have something for everyone. So come on in and let us take you on a culinary journey that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you feeling satisfied and nourished.

At Broth & Board, we believe that great food should be accompanied by great service. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff will provide you with an exceptional dining experience that will leave you feeling satisfied and eager to return. Whether you're looking for a quick lunch or a cozy dinner, Broth & Board is the perfect destination for food lovers of all kinds. Come and experience the flavors of our soups and charcuterie boards today!
